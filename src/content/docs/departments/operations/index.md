The Operations team exists to ensure Code Town Inc runs smoothly as a business.
We handle the non-engineering work that keeps the company healthy, compliant,
funded, and growing. Our mission is to remove friction, provide clarity, and
enable the engineering and product organizations to focus on building and
shipping great products.

## Core Principals

- **Bias for clarity** — Make information discoverable and processes explicit.
- **Default to automation** — If we do it more than twice, we automate or
  template it.
- **Lightweight, not bureaucratic** — Create only the process needed, never
  more.
- **Small team, big output** — Operate like a force multiplier for the entire
  company.

## Responsibilities

### Business Operations

Owns the systems and processes that keep the company running day-to-day. This
includes:

- Managing vendor relationships, subscriptions, and SaaS tooling.
- Supporting the sales and GTM motion through pricing, contracts, and deal
  operations
- Handling customer billing and subscription management in Stripe
- Responding to enterprise security questionnaires and due diligence requests.

Business Ops is also responsible for strategic projects that don't naturally
belong to engineering or product, including offsite planning, internal
documentation, and cross-functional initiatives.

### Finance & Accounting

Manages the financial health of the company. Responsibilities include
bookkeeping and monthly close, accounts payable and receivable, expense
management, and tax compliance across federal and state obligations. Finance
also owns relationships with external accountants and any state or regulatory
filings. For most financial decisions, we work with external partners and
maintain internal visibility through lightweight reporting.

### People Operations

Supports the team from hire to offboard. This includes coordinating hiring
pipelines, managing onboarding and offboarding checklists, administering
benefits, and maintaining employee records. People Ops also owns the employee
handbook and ensures our policies stay current and clearly communicated. As a
small team, we keep people processes simple and human. The goal is a great
experience for every team member, not process for its own sake.

### Legal & Compliance

Handles contracts, legal review, and regulatory obligations. This includes:

- NDAs, MSAs, DPAs, and other customer or vendor agreements
- Ensuring the company meets applicable data privacy requirements (GDPR, CCPA,
  etc.)
- Managing any ongoing state and local compliance obligations
- Coordinating with outside counsel when specialized legal review is needed.

We aim to move quickly on routine agreements using standard templates,
escalating only when necessary.
